# Promptos de Assets para Google AI Studio - Catirobas do Inferno

> Fonte: extraido de `mapa gráfico.pdf`.
> Uso no roadmap: base para o pipeline de geracao de assets por IA, com manifestos, lotes de importacao e QA visual.

Este documento contém os prompts para a geração de assets visuais (expressões faciais e
cenário) baseados no Game Design Document (GDD) de "Catirobas do Inferno".

Estilo Visual Alvo: Animação clássica e exagerada de Tex Avery (reações extremas, olhos
saltando, queixos caindo, elasticidade e humor visual absurdo).



PARTE 1: Expressões Faciais dos Protagonistas (Sprite Sheet)
Este prompt gera uma folha de sprites focada nas reações exageradas dos quatro heróis
principais.

     A hand-drawn cartoon character expression sheet in the wild, exaggerated
     animation style of Tex Avery. The sheet is divided into four columns, one for each
     main protagonist of the RPG "Catirobas do Inferno".

     Column 1: Boleto (The 80s Horse Cavaleiro). A bipedal horse with a large mullet
     and sparkly neon clothes.
      ●​ Expression A: Overconfident, smug, toothy grin thinking he is "hot".
      ●​ Expression B: A classic Tex Avery "wild take" – eyes popping out of sockets in
         hearts shape, jaw dropping to the floor in lust.
      ●​ Expression C: Confused and insulted, looking at his broken wooden sword
         that he thinks is gold.
     Column 2: Porquinho (The Anxious Pig Mage). A small pig in oversized wizard
     robes holding a twig wand.
      ●​ Expression A: Trembling panic, sweating profusely with giant cartoon sweat
         drops, eyes wide in social anxiety.
      ●​ Expression B: Trying too hard to look cool while casting a fake Latin spell,
         biting his tongue in concentration.
      ●​ Expression C: Melted into a puddle of embarrassment after being rejected.
     Column 3: Meleca (The Humanoid Slime Tank). A green, translucent gelatinous
     blob with two simple eyes.
      ●​ Expression A: Clueless, happy, vacant stare with a goofy smile, dripping
         slime.
      ●​ Expression B: Sad gelatin wobble, looking at his own sticky hands wondering
         why he's single.
      ●​ Expression C: Distorted "squash and stretch" face reacting to taking damage,
         splattering momentarily.
     Column 4: Caveira (The Depressed Skeleton Assassin). An emo skeleton with
     a cracked skull and two bone daggers.
      ●​ Expression A: Deep, existential sigh, eye sockets rolled up in sarcasm.
      ●​ Expression B: Crying cartoon dust instead of tears, looking "dead inside".
      ●​ Expression C: A dry, sarcastic smirk while holding his daggers weakly.


PARTE 2: Expressões Faciais dos Antagonistas (Sprite Sheet)
Este prompt gera uma folha de sprites para os inimigos comuns e conceitos de chefes,
mantendo o estilo cômico.

     A cartoon expression sprite sheet for RPG antagonists in the frenetic, zany style
     of Tex Avery animation.

     Row 1: "Nice Guy" Enemy. A man in a "I'm Nice" t-shirt.
      ●​ Expression A: A fake, strained smile while holding a door open, veins popping
         on forehead.
      ●​ Expression B: Exaggerated crying tantrum, pounding fists on the floor
         shouting "BUT I'M NICE!".
      ●​ Expression C: Sly, manipulative sideways glance.
     Row 2: "Troll Incel" Enemy. A pixelated shadow figure behind a glowing
     computer screen.
      ●​ Expression A: Furious rage face, entirely red, steam blowing out of ears while
         typing furiously.
      ●​ Expression B: Smug, pixelated troll grin in the dark.
      ●​ Expression C: Shrinking away in fear when confronted with reality.
     Row 3: "Homem Árvore" (Boss). A grumpy, gnarled tree face.
      ●​ Expression A: Scowling wooden face, refusing dermaabrasion.
      ●​ Expression B: Laughing cruelly with wooden teeth.
     Row 4: "Senhora Razão" (Final Boss Concept).
      ●​ Since she is an invisible concept of logic, represent her expressions as giant,
         glowing, ethereal punctuation marks in the air that convey extreme judgment:
         A massive, disapproving arched eyebrow made of light, and a giant, glowing,
         cold question mark questioning the heroes' life choices.


PARTE 3: Criação de Cenário - Taverna do Lamento
Este prompt cria o local inicial do jogo, usando perspectiva distorcida e humor visual para
transmitir a depressão do ambiente.

      A wide-angle cartoon background illustration of the "Taverna do Lamento"
      (Tavern of Lament), rendered in the warped, exaggerated perspective and distinct
      style of a Tex Avery cartoon.

      The tavern interior is dark, sticky, and incredibly grimy, looking like it hasn't been
      cleaned in years. The whole room feels depressed; tables are sagging in the
      middle, barstools are bent, and thick cartoon cobwebs hang like curtains
      everywhere.

      Key Elements visible in the scene:
       ●​ In a dark corner, a cartoon woman is sitting with her back completely turned
          to the camera, radiating a visible, jagged aura of "IGNORING EVERYONE".
       ●​ Near the bar, the NPC Zezé (the stoner sage) is floating imposibbly upside
          down in a thick cloud of comical green smoke, looking relaxed.
       ●​ A bored bartender stands behind a dirty counter covered in questionable
          bottles.
       ●​ The color palette is moody, with dirty browns, sickly greens, and dim lighting,
          but with bold cartoon outlines. The atmosphere is humorously pathetic.
